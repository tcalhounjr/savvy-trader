SAVVY TRADER

Savvy Trader is a good, solid stock market simulator that serves as an excellent practice tool for making actual investments and trying out various trading strategies. Additionally, it offers the opportunity to learn and master finance and investing basics, such as factoring in commissions and fees, shorting stocks, and utilizing popular analytical tools such as financial ratios like price/earnings and debt/equity. With this tool, the user will have a beginning balance of $100,000 to buy and sell stocks as they wish.
